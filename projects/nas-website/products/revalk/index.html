Exam-Proximity tiering

Know what is worth your time.

Every concept unit is tagged by exam proximity using PYQ topic mapping and frequency signals. This is not a quality rank. It is a relevance rank.

Iron

Useful concept

Not seen in PYQ topic history yet.

Bronze

Context builder

Common in Qbank ecosystems and helpful for coverage.

Silver

Bridge concept

Connects and supports PYQ-linked topics.

Gold

Directly exam-linked

Asked before or directly derivable from PYQ topic history.

Platinum

Repeated cluster

High-frequency PYQ topics that recur across years.
