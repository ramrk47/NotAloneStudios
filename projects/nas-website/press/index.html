Products

One-paragraph descriptions

Maulya

Maulya is a workflow operating system for valuation teams. It gives ops desks, field users, and external associates one disciplined surface for assignment intake, evidence capture, approvals, billing visibility, and controlled release. Over 2,400 assignments processed in production. Built by Not Alone Studios.

Revalk

Revalk is a premium study operating system for NEET/INI aspirants. It ranks concepts by PYQ topic proximity (Iron through Platinum), converts doubts into Walk Packs with audio and flashcards, and trains recall under pressure. Tablet-first, calm interface, no feed clutter. Currently in early access.

About Not Alone Studios

Not Alone Studios is a systems product studio that builds premium operator-grade software. Both flagship products (Maulya and Revalk) were built in-house using the same method: deterministic workflows, evidence-first design, and release controls that prevent incomplete delivery. The Studio lane offers selective custom systems builds for operations-heavy workflows.
